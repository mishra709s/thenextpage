About Us

The Next Page is specialized in Corporate Merchandise. For our clients it translates into path breaking concepts and move their brands miles ahead of competition.

We offer a professional and reliable service for the provision of all your business gifts and promotional products, from artwork generation through to sourcing, production and distribution. With over 5000 products in our gifting ideas, every year we bring you the widest variety of gifts to choose from. For the brand conscious customer, there is basket of brands that we offer. With a team committed to service, we are constantly leveraging our potential to source from all over Asia to benefit our customers. Our team comprises of in-house graphic designers, a dedicated procurement team, operations, marketing, sales and finance.

Promotional products and business gifts are the most cost effective way of promoting your company and, as a leading supplier; we have tens of thousands of corporate gift ideas, marketing merchandise and promotional items available.
